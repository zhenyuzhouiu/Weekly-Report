\section{Finger Nail Blood Vessel Key Points Detection}

Last week, I trained the YOLOV5 based on the only bifurcation detection model to detect bifurcation and ending at the same time. I have split the total 84 images to 75 images and 9 images, the former treated as training set, and the latter treated as testing set. In this kind of situation, I don't have the validation set.

From the above AP matrix, the bifurcation detection accuracy is very low. On the "Only Bifurcation Detection Model", the maximum AP only can get 35.021\%. When the model continue to learn the ending point, the AP will drop about 2\%. Although I have used the Focal Loss to balance the loss from unbalance positive sample from different classes.
